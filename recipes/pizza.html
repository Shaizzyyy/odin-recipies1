Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Here we have a beautiful succulent pepperoni pizza. This dish is originated from Italy of course, with many worldwide variations. Today we are focusing on a simple, yet amazing tasting pepperoni pizza from the United States. Pizza is known to be a favourite dish of many different people. It is something that is hard to not ABSOLUTELY love if you are a normal human being! With all that said, we can get started on the recipe, beginning with the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Grocery Pizza Dough
  • Sliced Pepperoni
  • Shredded Mozzerelle & Cheddar Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Flatten pizza dough into a circle with roller, or by hands, creating a flat center, and creating a crust-like border around the perimeter.
  2. Pour out enough Tomato sauce onto the pizza doguh, and use a spoon to evenly spread out the sauce in the middle of the pizza.
  3. Drizzle shredded cheese onto the tomato sauce on the pizza dough.
  4. Spread pepperoni slices evenly across the cheese,however you desire.
  5. Bake pizza inside over at 350 degrees until pizza crust is golden brown, and cheese is melted to preference
  6. Wait 5 min for pizza to cool before cutting, then enjoy!
