Grilled Cheese Recipe

Recipe Discription

Here we have a beautiful succulent Grilled Cheese. This dish is originated from America, with many worldwide variations. Today we are focusing on a simple, yet amazing tasting GRilled Cheese from the United States. Grilled Cheese is known to be a favourite dish of many different people. It is something that is hard to not ABSOLUTELY love if you are a normal human being! With all that said, we can get started on the recipe, beginning with the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • American sliced cheese
  • Your choice of butter
  • Frying pan of your choice

Directions

  1. Spread butter evenly across one side of bread, then repeat for the other slice.
  2. Place two slices of cheese in between the bread, on the non buttered sides.
  3. Spread butter on frying pan, then place pan on stove with medium heat.
  4. Place cheese sandwhich on pan, until golden brown on one side.
  5. Once golden brown, flip to the other side.
  6. Once golden gronw on both sides, take grilled cheese of pan, and enjoy!
